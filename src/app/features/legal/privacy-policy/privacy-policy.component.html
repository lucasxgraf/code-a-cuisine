General information

The following information provides a simple overview of what happens to your personal data when you visit this website. Personal data is any data by which you can be personally identified. Detailed information on data protection can be found in our privacy policy listed below.

Data collection on this website

Who is responsible for data collection on this website?

Data processing on this website is carried out by the website operator. You can find the operator’s contact details in the section “Information on the responsible party” in this privacy policy.

How do we collect your data?

On the one hand, your data is collected when you provide it to us. This may, for example, be data that you enter into a form (e.g., ingredients for recipe generation).

Other data is collected automatically or after your consent when you visit the website by our IT systems. This is mainly technical data (e.g., internet browser, operating system, or time of page access).

What do we use your data for?

Some of the data is collected to ensure the website is provided without errors. Other data is used to provide the core functions of the app (AI recipe generation, storage of favorites/likes).

What rights do you have regarding your data?

You have the right at any time to obtain information free of charge about the origin, recipients, and purpose of your stored personal data. You also have the right to request the correction or deletion of this data. Furthermore, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority.

4. Specific Features of this App

Use of Artificial Intelligence (AI) for Recipe Generation

Nature and purpose of processing: Users can generate recipe suggestions on this website. For this purpose, we use Artificial Intelligence (AI). The ingredients and preferences you enter are processed to create individual recipe ideas.

Data flow and third-party providers: To provide this feature, your input is transmitted to external service providers:

  • Groq: AI model provider for generating recipes.
  • n8n Cloud: Workflow automation to manage data between the app and AI.
  • Spoonacular API: Service for ingredient autocompletion.
  • Supabase: Database for storing generated recipes in the "cookbook".

Legal basis: Processing is based on Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR (performance of a contract/user relationship) and Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in functionality).

International data transfers: Since some providers (e.g., Groq, Vercel, Supabase) are based in the USA, data may be transferred to a third country. This is based on Standard Contractual Clauses (SCC) of the EU Commission.

Storage of Likes (LocalStorage & UUID)

When you "like" recipes, we store a randomly generated identifier (UUID) in your browser (LocalStorage). This UUID is stored in our database (Supabase) along with the like. This serves to prevent duplicate likes and to display your favorites upon return without requiring a login.

Storage in LocalStorage is based on § 25(2) TDDDG (strictly necessary for the function explicitly requested by the user) or Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR.
